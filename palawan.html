Island Bliss: Tales from My Palawan Tails

Palawan, often called the Last Frontier of the Philippines, is a true paradise that exceeded all my expectations.

From its pristine beaches to its lush green landscapes, every moment felt like a scene from a dream. Let me take you on a journey through some of my unforgettable experiences and must-try activities on this stunning island.

My Experience in Palawan The journey began the moment I arrived. The island's charm greeted me with its warm breeze and turquoise waters.

I explored El Nido and its breathtaking limestone cliffs, hopping from one hidden beach to another.

The clear waters of Big Lagoon were perfect for kayaking, offering me a front-row view of nature's wonders.

In Coron, I swam in the surreal Kayangan Lake, said to be the cleanest lake in the Philippines. Its crystal-clear waters reflect the sky like a giant mirror.

Hiking up to the viewpoint was a bit of a challenge, but the view? Worth every step.

Of course, no trip to Palawan is complete without visiting the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park.

Gliding through the underground river felt like a trip into another world, with its stunning rock formations and unique ecosystem.
